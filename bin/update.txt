Enter a new distance by clicking on the Escape Distance menu.

Enter a number greater than zero in the JOptionPane InputDialogBox.

Enter a new maximum escape time by clicking on the Max Escape Time menu.

Enter a number greater than zero and less than 256 in the JOptionPane InputDialogBox.


If you enter a number that is not valid or a string, then the MessageDialogBox will pop up because a new 
IllegalArgumentException is thrown and it will ask you to enter another valid input. Then that same method is called so that it will 
only accept a legal value or close if you cancel or exit the JOptionPane.
